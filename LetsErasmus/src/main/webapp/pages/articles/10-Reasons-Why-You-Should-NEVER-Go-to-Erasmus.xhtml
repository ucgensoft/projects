10 Reasons Why You Should NEVER Go to Erasmus!

As a challenging mobility program of European Commission, Erasmus is an exchange opportunity which offers study abroad where you can receive grant within the limits of the program. Surely there are many reasons to go for this challenge. Here you can find why you should NEVER go to Erasmus regarding following 10 reasons;

If you think that creating bigger and better network is not useful, you shouldn't go to Erasmus since it is all about meeting new people who can create new opportunities for you.

Samantha Gades

If you say that it is not a benefit to have an abroad experience in my CV, don't bother to go Erasmus.

Tim Gouw

If you say I love my culture and I don't want to know others, and I love the close box I live, you should never go to Erasmus. Erasmus welcomes all cultures who wants to discover and experience it.

Anthony DELANOIX

Just like culture, developing your language skills is another opportunity that Erasmus program offers. If you say I'm fine with my language skills, you should never go to Erasmus.

Jelleke Vanooteghem

If you say you have enough friends for a lifetime, why bother to go Erasmus?

It is a fact that Erasmus teaches people to take responsibility and learn to be independent, but who likes independency right?

If you say my world is my home, you should never go to Erasmus since it is all about travelling and discovering more places.

Matheus Ferrero

While you live abroad, life gives you more self-experience to know yourself better and maybe discover your hidden characteristics. But, you shouldn't bother to go if you don't want to know yourself better.
